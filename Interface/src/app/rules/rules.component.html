Basic Rules

Welcome to the game of Kassen.

1

You are only allowed to take a piss with the piss card.
If you do want to go, it's gonna cost you a shot!
This shot does not count for your drinking buddies!

2

The amount that you are drinking is based on the difficulty of the game.
This difficulty is player specific and can be change during the game.
Upon starting the game, you will roll the dice to determine the starting difficulty.

3

Every Strike you get is an automatic +2 Difficulty!
